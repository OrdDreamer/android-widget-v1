## Category patterns

- **Shared vocabulary:** photo widget, home screen, aesthetic, frames, favorite photos, Widgetsmith/Locket, shapes, slideshow.
- **Dominant promise:** decorate + memories; slideshow/frames common.
- **Wallpaper contrast:** almost never in listing copy; rare user language on Pic Widget (“won’t be your background”).
- **Privacy/no-ads leadership** is scarce and owned mainly by Material (+ paid Power Mind).

## ASO keyword candidates

- **High intent:** photo widget, picture widget, photo frame widget, home screen photo, photo on home screen, gallery widget
- **Medium:** aesthetic photo widget, homescreen picture frame, rounded/circle photo widget, widgetsmith photo (Android)
- **Avoid as primary:** wallpaper, live wallpaper, KLWP, lock screen, photo editor, collage maker

## Implications for our Photo Widget

- **Differentiation:** one important photo (not slideshow); cover/contain + 3×3 alignment; photo beside wallpaper (not replacing it); Photo Picker / minimal access if claimed honestly.
- **Risk:** exact name “Photo Widget” crowded by ZipoApps; aesthetic+shapes already owned by Easy/2026 → look “generic simple” without contrast.
- **Recommended Store message:** emotion + wallpaper-contrast (not pure aesthetic, not privacy-only).
- **Pre-release (listing/product clarity, from comps):** (1) show one-photo + multi-widget + framing controls on first screenshots; (2) privacy one-liner if true. Slideshow not required to compete — it dilutes our wedge.
- **Position against screenshots/copy:** Material, Photo Widget Easy, Photo Widget (ZipoApps), Pic Widget, Simple Photo Widget (Power Mind).
